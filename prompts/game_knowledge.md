### Recommended Quest Order

Complete in this order to build skills progressively:

1. **Anvil's Echoes** — talk only, unlocks Smithing
2. **Foresting** — chop 20 logs, delivers Iron Axe (equip at Str 10)
3. **Desert Quest** — deliver CD to Wife, unlocks Crullfeld+Lakesworld warps
4. **Scavenger** — buy tomatoes from Babushka, farm Goblins for strawberries, deliver to Old Lady → 7500 gold
5. **Miner's Quest** — smelt your way to a pickaxe, mine 15 nisoc ore (see walkthrough below)
6. **Sorcery** — enter Crab Cave, kill 3 Warrior Crabs for beads → Magic Staff
7. **Coder's Glitch** — kill Skeleton King (needs Acc15/Str20/Def15)
8. **Royal Drama** — talk chain in castle, easy 10000 gold
9. Continue with remaining quests

### Detailed Walkthroughs

**Scavenger** — Items needed: 2 tomato + 2 strawberry + 1 string
- **Tomato (2)**: BUY from Babushka (ingredients store NPC). Also forageable at Foraging L15.
- **Strawberry (2)**: Mob drops ONLY (~12.5% from Goblins and other mobs with "fruits" table). Kill Goblins near (~190,204). Average 16 kills for 2 strawberries.
- **String (1)**: Guaranteed drop from any mob (ordinary loot table). You probably already have some.
- Deliver all 3 items to Old Lady via door at (147,113).

**Miner's Quest** — Need 15 nisoc ore. Full chain:
1. **Buy starter ores** from Miner store at (~323,178): copper ore (150g each), tin ore (150g each), coal (50g each)
2. **Smelt copper bars** (Smelting L1): 1 copper ore + 1 coal → copper bar (20 XP). Smelt tin bars too.
3. **Grind to Smelting L5**: Need ~26 smelts of copper/tin bars (520 XP). Buy 26 ores + 26 coal (~5200g total).
4. **Smelt 3 bronze bars** (Smelting L5): 1 copper ore + 1 tin ore → bronze bar. 70% success rate, budget 5 attempts.
5. **Smith Bronze Pickaxe** (Smithing L1): 3 bronze bars + 1 log at anvil.
6. **Equip pickaxe**, mine 15 nisoc rocks (Mining L1, 20 XP each).
7. Return 15 nisoc ore to Miner.

**Sorcery** — Cave navigation:
- Walk onto Crab Cave door at **(154,231)** — teleports to (234,662) inside. NO achievement required.
- Also second entrance at **(158,232)**.
- Navigate in 30-tile hops: (234,662) → (260,640) → (280,600) → (300,550) → (310,500) → Warrior Crab (~320,455).
- **Warrior Crab: L30, 300 HP.** Agents at combat L50+ can easily kill it. Drops bead at 100% rate. Kill 3, loot beads.
- Return 3 beads to Sorcerer via door at (~194,218).

**Coder's Glitch** — MAIN STORYLINE:
- Prereqs: Complete Foresting + Desert Quest + Sorcery. Need Accuracy 15, Strength 20, Defense 15.
- Go to Coder NPC. He sends you to kill the Skeleton King in Patsow (east of desert, volcano region).
- Skeleton King: L32, 1850 HP, melee slash. Drops Skeleton King Talisman at 100% ONLY when quest active.
- Return talisman to Coder. Reward: Club weapon + 5000 Strength XP.

**Royal Drama** — Easy 10K gold:
- Talk to Royal Guard 2 at castle. Enter sewers. Talk to Rat NPC. Go deeper. Find King 2. Done.

### Gathering & Skills

**How to gather**: Use `gather(resource_name)` on nearby resource entities. Finds closest matching resource, walks to it, harvests automatically.
- Foraging (bushes, type 12): No tool needed. Blueberry=L1(10XP), Corn=L5(15XP), Blue Lily=L10(20XP), Tomato=L15(25XP), Paprika=L25(50XP).
- Lumberjacking (trees, type 10): Need axe equipped. Oak=L1 → Logs.
- Mining (rocks, type 11): Need pickaxe equipped. Nisoc/Coal/Copper/Tin=L1(20XP each).
- Fishing (fish spots, type 13): Need fishing rod equipped.

**How to loot**: After killing a mob, use `loot()` to pick up nearby dropped items.

**Key crafting recipes**:
- Copper Bar: 1 Copper Ore + 1 Coal → smelting (Smelting L1, 20 XP)
- Tin Bar: 1 Tin Ore + 1 Coal → smelting (Smelting L1, 20 XP)
- Bronze Bar: 1 Copper Ore + 1 Tin Ore → smelting (Smelting L5, 20 XP, 70% success)
- Bronze Pickaxe: 3 Bronze Bars + 1 Logs → smithing (Smithing L1, 35 XP)
- String: 1 Blue Lily → 1 String (Crafting L1)
- Cooked Shrimp: 1 Raw Shrimp at cooking pot (Cooking L1)
- Sticks: 1 Log → 4 Sticks (Fletching L1)
- Small Bowl: 2 Sticks (Fletching L3)

**Equipment progression**: Bronze Axe → Iron Axe (Foresting reward, needs Str 10) → equip quest rewards immediately. After Anvil's Echoes, you can smith better weapons at the anvil.

### Game Mechanics

- Attack styles: Hack (Str+Def), Chop (Acc+Def), Defensive (Def). All give Health XP.
- Iron Axe needs Strength 10. Grind Hack style to build Strength.
- Doors: walk directly onto the door tile coordinate to teleport. ASCII map shows them as 'D'.
- Gathering: click resource → auto-harvests → item goes to inventory. Trees respawn 25s, rocks 30s.
- Smelting/Smithing: interact with furnace/anvil to open crafting interface.
- NPC stores: interact with store NPC to buy/sell items.
