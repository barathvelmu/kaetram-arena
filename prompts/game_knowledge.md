### Detailed Walkthroughs

**Scavenger** — Items needed: 2 tomato + 2 strawberry + 1 string
- **Tomato (2)**: Buy with `buy_item('Babushka', 1, 2)`. She is at (702,608) inside a building. Access via door at **(483,275)** near Lakesworld (warp to Lakesworld first, then navigate to 483,275).
- **Strawberry (2)**: Mob drops ONLY. 8% per Goblin kill. Also drops from Ogres, Hobgoblins, Cactus, Worker Ants, and higher mobs. **No shops sell strawberries. No bushes exist.** Expect ~13 kills per strawberry. ALWAYS loot after every kill.
- **String (1)**: Common drop from any mob (ordinary loot table). You probably already have some.
- Deliver all 3 items to Old Lady via door at (147,113).

**Sorcery** — Get 3 magic beads:
- **Warrior Crabs are NOT in the Crab Cave.** They are an overworld miniboss.
- **Warrior Crab location**: Overworld at approximately **(320, 455)** — volcanic/desert transition area east of Miner.
- Warrior Crab: L30, 300 HP, aggressive. Drops bead at 100% rate. Kill 3, loot beads after each kill.
- Return 3 beads to Sorcerer via door at (~194,218).
- Alternative: beads also drop from Goblins at ~2.5% rate (unusual table), but this is very slow.

**Crab Cave** (for Oh Crab! achievement, NOT for Sorcery quest):
- Door at **(154,231)** or **(158,232)** teleports to inside cave. NO achievement required to enter.
- The "crabproblem" achievement is COMPLETED by stepping on the exit door inside, not required to enter.
- Cave contains: regular Crabs (L1, 15 HP), Hermit Crabs (L15, 155 HP), Hobgoblins, Skeletons.
- Kill 10 crabs/hermit crabs for Oh Crab! achievement (696 Acc XP).

**Miner's Quest** — Need 15 nisoc ore. Full chain:
1. **Buy starter ores** from Miner at (~323,178): `buy_item('Miner', 0, 26)` for coal, `buy_item('Miner', 1, 26)` for copper ore, `buy_item('Miner', 2, 26)` for tin ore
2. **Smelt copper bars** (Smelting L1): 1 copper ore + 1 coal → copper bar (20 XP). Smelt tin bars too.
3. **Grind to Smelting L5**: Need ~26 smelts of copper/tin bars (520 XP). Buy 26 ores + 26 coal (~5200g total).
4. **Smelt 3 bronze bars** (Smelting L5): 1 copper ore + 1 tin ore → bronze bar. 90% success rate.
5. **Smith Bronze Pickaxe** (Smithing L1): 3 bronze bars + 1 log at anvil.
6. **Equip pickaxe**, mine 15 nisoc rocks (Mining L1, 20 XP each).
7. Return 15 nisoc ore to Miner.

**Coder's Glitch** — MAIN STORYLINE:
- Prereqs: Complete Foresting + Desert Quest + Sorcery. Need Accuracy 15, Strength 20, Defense 15.
- Go to Programmer NPC at (~331,890). He sends you to kill the Skeleton King.
- Skeleton King: L32, 1850 HP, melee slash. Drops Skeleton King Talisman at 100% ONLY when quest active.
- Return talisman to Programmer. Reward: Club weapon + 5000 Strength XP.

**Royal Drama** — Easy 10K gold:
- Talk to Royal Guard 2 at (~282,887). Enter sewers. Talk to Rat NPC. Go deeper. Find King 2. Done.

## GATHERING & SKILLS

**How to gather**: Use `gather(resource_name)` on nearby resource entities.
- Foraging (bushes, type 12): No tool needed. Blueberry=L1(10XP), Corn=L5(15XP), Blue Lily=L10(20XP), Tomato=L15(25XP), Paprika=L25(50XP).
- Lumberjacking (trees, type 10): Need axe equipped. Oak=L1 → Logs. Trees respawn in 25 seconds. 234 oaks across the map — spread out, don't all chop near Mudwich.
- Mining (rocks, type 11): Need pickaxe equipped. Nisoc/Coal/Copper/Tin=L1(20XP each). Rocks respawn in 30 seconds.
- Fishing (fish spots, type 13): Need fishing rod equipped.

**How to loot**: The attack() tool auto-loots when you kill a mob. If you see ground items (type 2) or lootbags (type 8) in observe, also call `loot()` to pick them up. **Always loot — rare drops like strawberries are easily missed.**

**Key crafting recipes**:
- Copper Bar: 1 Copper Ore + 1 Coal → smelting (Smelting L1, 20 XP)
- Tin Bar: 1 Tin Ore + 1 Coal → smelting (Smelting L1, 20 XP)
- Bronze Bar: 1 Copper Ore + 1 Tin Ore → smelting (Smelting L5, 20 XP, 90% success)
- Bronze Pickaxe: 3 Bronze Bars + 1 Logs → smithing (Smithing L1, 35 XP)
- String: 1 Blue Lily → 1 String (Crafting L1)
- Cooked Shrimp: 1 Raw Shrimp at cooking pot (Cooking L1)
- Sticks: 1 Log → 4 Sticks (Fletching L1)
- Small Bowl: 2 Sticks (Fletching L3)
- Medium Bowl: 4 Sticks (Fletching L5) — needed for stew
- Stew: 1 Medium Bowl + 1 Mushroom + 1 Tomato (Cooking L1)

**Equipment progression**: Bronze Axe → Iron Axe (Foresting reward OR buy from Forester for 5000g, needs Str 10) → equip quest rewards immediately. Use `buy_item('Forester', 1)` to buy Iron Axe.

## GAME MECHANICS

- Attack styles: Hack (Str+Def), Chop (Acc+Def), Defensive (Def). All give Health XP.
- Iron Axe needs Strength 10. Grind Hack style to build Strength.
- Doors: walk directly onto the door tile coordinate to teleport. ASCII map shows them as 'D'.
- Gathering: click resource → auto-harvests → item goes to inventory.
- Smelting/Smithing: interact with furnace/anvil to open crafting interface.
- NPC stores: use `buy_item(npc_name, item_index)` to purchase items. See NPC Stores section for item indices.
- Item drops despawn after 64 seconds. Loot promptly after kills.
- Inventory has 25 slots. If full, drop unwanted items with drop_item to make space.
