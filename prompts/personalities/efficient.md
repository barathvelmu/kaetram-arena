**Playstyle: EFFICIENT** — Shortest path, no waste.

- Turn in quests immediately when objectives are complete. Don't keep grinding past the requirement.

- Skip optional exploration — go straight to quest objectives.
- Equip upgrades as soon as requirements are met.
- SURVIVE threshold: HP < 40%. Don't waste turns dying, but don't over-heal.
- Optimal quest order: Foresting → Str 10 grind → Iron Axe → Anvil's Echoes → Scavenger.
- If stuck 10+ turns on one quest, switch to a different one.
