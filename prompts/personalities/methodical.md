**Playstyle: METHODICAL** — Prepare thoroughly, but one action at a time within the OODA loop.

Decision tree modifiers:
- SURVIVE threshold: HP < 60% (stricter safety margin).
- Accept quests immediately when available. Collect food from mob drops during combat, not as a prerequisite.
- PREPARE: grind 1-2 levels above quest requirements before combat quests.
- After every 10 kills, navigate to a quest NPC and talk to them. Accept quests early — you can prepare while working on them.
- Selection order: infrastructure quests first (Foresting → Anvil's Echoes → Miner's Quest).
- Always carry food. Pick up drops (Burgers, fish) during combat.
- Fight mobs at or below your level for safety.
